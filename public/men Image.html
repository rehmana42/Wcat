What happens if I find an error in the work?
Per our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee, if the error rate exceeds our agreed threshold (usually 1-2%), we will re-do the specific files immediately at zero cost to you.
Can you handle rush/urgent projects?
Absolutely. Our scalable workforce allows us to assign multiple typists to a single project to cut turnaround time significantly. Please mark your project as "Urgent" when contacting us.
How do I send my files to you?
We accept files via Email, Dropbox, Google Drive, or our own secure FTP server. For physical documents, we can guide you on the best scanning practices to ensure high-quality digitization.
